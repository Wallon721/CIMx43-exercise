THE STAR FORGER

Stars, shining so beautiful,
My creations, those were.
Everything you see, I created,
But suddenly I was, caged.

On a planet with much magic,
Those who I made revolted,
And they made a crown
With a deep curse for me.

My powers, almost gone.
Trust was shattered,
Freedom, forgot what it is
Solitude took over me like poison.

As the time passed slowly,
Hope began to perish
And I stayed there quietly.
It was nightmarish to bear the pain.

Years went by, and still here I am.
No one willing to help me,
But I found a new purpose,
Trying to break the golden crown.
